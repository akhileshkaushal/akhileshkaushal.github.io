RNAseq-Pipeline

RNA-seq Transcriptomics DESeq2

Description

A reproducible and modular pipeline for RNA-seq analysis including quality control, alignment with STAR, quantification with Salmon, differential expression with DESeq2, and GSEA-based enrichment analysis. Designed for bulk RNA-seq but modular to adapt for others.

Steps Included

  • Quality control with FastQC and MultiQC
  • Alignment with STAR (or pseudoalignment with Salmon)
  • Transcript quantification and count matrix generation
  • Differential expression with DESeq2
  • Pathway enrichment (GSEA, ORA)

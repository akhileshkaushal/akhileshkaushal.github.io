Whole-Exome Sequencing in Relapsing CLL

This documentation covers the bioinformatics workflow, modernized pipeline recommendations, and key genomic findings from Ljungström et al., Blood (2016), focusing on whole-exome sequencing (WES) of chronic lymphocytic leukemia (CLL) in pre-treatment and relapse settings.

The case study provides: - Scientific context and study hypothesis - Original bioinformatics methodology - Modernized WES pipeline recommendations - Key genomic findings and interpretation guidelines - Diagrammatic overview of historical vs modern workflows

Note

While the original study used a legacy pipeline (BWA-MEM to hg19, GATK3, VarScan2 with 10% VAF cutoff), this documentation updates all methods to current best practices (BWA-MEM2, GRCh38, GATK4 Mutect2, lower VAF thresholds).
